Temporary Calculation of the amount of Old - Age Pension

Here you have the possibility of proceeding to a temporary calculation of the amount of old-age pension.

Prerequisites for the execution of eService

1. You will need to register at Cy Login. New user? Register here

2. Already a registered user? You will also need to authenticate your profile through your electronic banking system (eBanking) or by visiting a Citizen Service Centre or a Citizen Center (KE.PO. - District Post Offices).

3. Have you been registered and authenticated? Please enroll in the system of the Social Insurance Services here
